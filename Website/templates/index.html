What We Solve

Smarter Water Use, Better Plant Care

In traditional irrigation, plants often receive too much water or not enough water. Our project monitors soil moisture, temperature, and humidity in real time, giving users clear data so irrigation decisions become easier and more accurate.

Less Manual Checking

The user does not need to visit the field again and again to check soil conditions. Live dashboard values make plant conditions easier to understand.

Protection From Overwatering

Too much water can damage roots, wash away soil nutrients, and waste water. Sensor data helps avoid unnecessary irrigation.

Better Plant Growth

When plants receive water at the right time and in the right conditions, growth improves. This project gives users a quick view of plant health.

Without This Project

What problems can happen without monitoring?

Without soil moisture and environmental data, irrigation depends on guesswork. This can increase water waste, plant stress, low crop quality, and manual effort.

  • Unnecessary water usage can increase
  • Plants can become weak when soil stays dry
  • Overwatering can damage plant roots
  • Users do not get a live view of field conditions
